Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
The short version: we collect only what we need to make the app work. We don't sell your data. Ever.

1. Who we are

VisaStay Tracker is a web application that helps non-EU travelers track their stay in the Schengen Area. It is operated by VisaStay Tracker.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: [email protected]

2. What data we collect

Data you provide directly:

  • Trip data — country, entry date, exit date. This is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and never sent to our servers unless you create an account.
  • Email address — if you join the app waitlist or create an account. We use this only to send you relevant updates about the product.

Data collected automatically:

  • Analytics data — we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how people use the app (pages visited, buttons clicked, session duration). This data is anonymized and aggregated. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect this information.
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, and approximate location (country level) collected automatically by our hosting provider (Vercel).

3. Why we collect it (legal basis)

  • Trip data — to provide the core functionality of the app. Legal basis: contract performance / legitimate interest.
  • Email address — to send you updates you have explicitly requested. Legal basis: consent.
  • Analytics data — to improve the product and understand usage patterns. Legal basis: legitimate interest.

4. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Functional cookies — to store your trips locally in your browser (localStorage). These are strictly necessary for the app to work.
  • Analytics cookies — set by Google Analytics 4 to track usage patterns. You can opt out by declining cookies when prompted, or by using browser extensions like uBlock Origin.

We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertisers.

5. How long we keep your data

  • Trip data in localStorage — until you clear your browser data or delete the trips manually.
  • Email addresses (waitlist) — until you ask us to remove them.
  • Analytics data — retained by Google Analytics for 14 months (Google's default).

6. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the following third-party services that help us operate the app:

  • Google Analytics (analytics) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Vercel (hosting) — Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Supabase (database, future) — Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Stripe (payments, future) — Stripe Privacy Policy

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — you can ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. Trip data is stored only in your browser unless you create an account. When account features are live, all data will be transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored in secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase).

9. Children

VisaStay Tracker is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
